Robograder

About us

Robograder is a revolutionary automated essay scoring system developed by a student at the Asia Pacific University. Robograder is a sophisticated tool that can accurately grade essays written by learners at both the lower secondary and upper secondary levels. The system relies on a powerful Random Forest algorithm to analyze and accurately grade each essay. Robograder stands out among automated essay scoring systems because of its unparalleled accuracy and speed. The system can process and grade thousands of essays per day, ensuring that results are available quickly. Robograder is designed to make grading easier and faster for both teachers and students. Teachers can upload assignments to the platform and trust Robograder to accurately grade each assignment in a fraction of the time taken by manual grading. This should help teachers to save considerable time that can be used for other activities such as providing more personalized feedback to students. Robograder is also designed with students in mind. It provides students with clear and instant feedback on their essays so they can make improvements quickly and with greater confidence.
